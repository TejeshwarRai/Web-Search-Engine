The Scope of Our
Services














Discovery
The first step in the process and includes research and analysis to investigate how the website can best serve the needs of the business and its customers.








Performance

At Complere we deploy your website on the content delivery network (CDN) like Amazon CloudFront to improve the performance of your website by securely delivering data, videos, applications, and APIs to customers globally with low latency and high transfer speeds.









Reporting

We set up Analytics to track website activity such as session duration, pages per session, bounce rate, etc. of individuals using the site and the information on the source of the traffic.









Customization

We offer Website Customization Services which will not only offer you an entire solution program but is affordable as well for the long run.











Optimization
We Offer Website optimization using tools, advanced strategies, and experiments to improve the performance of your website, further drive more traffic, increase conversions, and grow revenue.








Backups

Complere helps you set up ad-hoc, custom schedules and automatic secure backup of your websites.









Security
With SSL Certificates(SSL), Web Application Firewall(WAF), Content delivery network(CDN), Monitoring, Multifactor Authentication, and regular backups implementation we ensure the security of your website








SEO
With a profound experience of having achieved top search engine rankings for clients hailing from different industry segments, our team of experienced and highly qualified SEO consultants, copywriters and digital marketers knows what it takes to make your website stand out and rise to the top of the search engines.
